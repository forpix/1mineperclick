What this site is

An unofficial, independent fan wiki for +1 Mine Per Click, the Roblox incremental mining simulator by the verified group VERY LUCKY (place 74193805629461, released May 17, 2026). A similarly named game by a different developer causes constant confusion, so precision is the founding job here — see the comparison page.

How facts get checked

Stats (players, visits, favorites) come from the public Roblox API and are date-stamped wherever they appear. Mechanics come from the game's official description and in-game observation; numbers that change with updates are described at the mechanics level rather than invented. Codes follow one rule: nothing is listed as working without evidence from an official source or an in-game redeem check — if the codes page says there are none, that is a checked fact.

What this site is not

Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation or VERY LUCKY. Not a script or exploit resource — we do not publish, link, or review them. Revenue, if any, comes from clearly-labeled advertising.
