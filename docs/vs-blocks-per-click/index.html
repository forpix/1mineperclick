+1 Mine Per Click vs +1 Blocks Per Click

Last updated: July 3, 2026

These two Roblox games get mixed up constantly — the names are near-identical, both are "+1 per click" incremental games, and search engines blend their results together. They are different games by different, unrelated developers. Here's the untangling.

Why the confusion matters

The practical damage is the codes mix-up. +1 Blocks Per Click has a real code-redeem system, so dozens of sites maintain code lists for it. When +1 Mine Per Click took off in mid-2026, those results started surfacing for Mine Per Click searches too — and people ended up typing Blocks Per Click codes into a game that, as far as we can verify, has no redeem box at all. If you came here from a codes list, the codes status page has the full story.

Which one are you looking for?

  • You're mining downward with a pickaxe, training Strength, and the game mentions rebirthing to "reach the bottom" → you're playing +1 Mine Per Click by VERY LUCKY. You're in the right place — start with the beginner's guide.
  • You're clicking for blocks, redeeming codes like UPDATE3, and the developer is Diamond Pickaxe Games → that's +1 Blocks Per Click, a different game this site doesn't cover.

There are also older lookalikes on Roblox (e.g. "+1 Mine Every Click") — checking the developer name on the game page is the reliable way to tell any of them apart.
